Most drinkware treats hydration as an afterthought. We treat it as infrastructure for modern life.

SIPPLE was created to rethink something simple: the way we drink water every day. Hydration is constant. It moves with us from desks to gyms, commutes to kitchens. Yet most drinkware has remained static, disconnected from the way modern routines actually work.

SIPPLE exists to close that gap. Not by adding features for complexity — but by solving the real friction points that interrupt hydration across every environment you move through.

Each form is sculpted with precision, balancing thermal performance with visual harmony. The result is drinkware that does not interrupt your environment — it elevates it.
